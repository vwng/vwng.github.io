PRETTY is an immersive interactive website dedicated to the works of 20th-century poet Sara Teasdale. The user experience replicates an intimate experience of "going through one's mail" to reflect the personal nature of her writing. Every element on the site can be moved around, thereby allowing each visitor to dictate an individual experience. PRETTY is an ode to Teasdale's love of flowers and all things beautiful.
